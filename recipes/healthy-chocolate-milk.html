Healthy Chocolate Milk

Luscious Chocolate drink that is beleived to be a gift from the Gods.

Other than its rich chocolate taste, it is packed with nutrients that helps you to be strong and healthy.

Ingredients

  • Cacao Powder---1.5 tbsp
  • Raw Goats milk--- 1 cup
  • Raw Honey--- 1 tbsp
  • 85% sugar free chocolates 1 tspn
  • Large eggs---2 pcs

Directions

  1. Crack your eggs and separate the yolks from the whites.
  2. Whisk the yolks in the saucepan and add Raw Goats milk.
  3. Warm the mixture in low heat for about 1min and 50secs.
  4. Put the Cacao powder in your favorite mug.
  5. Add the warm milk gradually to avoid Cacao powder solids.
  6. Add the Raw Honey and stir it until it blends with the drink.
  7. Shave your 85% sugar free chocolate on top.

Note: You can add the eggs whites if you prefer or you can use it on other Dishes.
