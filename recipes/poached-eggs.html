Poached Eggs

An Enticing Egg Dish, which you will love at first sight

A nutrition packed meal that will provide eneregy for the whole day.

It looks easy to make but will need a lot of effort to perfect.

Ingredients

  • Eggs---2/3 pcs
  • Your choice of bread
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Butter---2 tbsp
  • Vinegar---1 tbsp
  • Water---3/4 cups

Directions

  1. Boil your water in a caserole.
  2. While waiting for the water to boil, slice your bread and apply some butter.
  3. Toast your bread
  4. Pour the vinegar into the water.
  5. Crack your eggs and put it into a small bowl
  6. Stir the water until a vortex have formed
  7. Add your eggs slowly into the swirling water.
  8. Take out your eggs after cooking it for at least 3 mins.
  9. Lay it on top of your toasted bread
  10. Add salt and pepper to taste.
