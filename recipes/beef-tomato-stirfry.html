Beef and Tomato Stir-fry

Mouth watering Beef and Tomato Dish

Has a sour and lightly sweet taste that will make you want for more!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef---.25Kgs
  • Tomatoes---5pcs
  • Onions---1pc large
  • Garlic---1 Clove
  • Green Bell Pepper---1pc
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Beef tallow or any healthy cooking oils---1Tbsp
  • Cooked Rice

Directions

  1. Dice your garlic and onions
  2. Heat up your caserole and put in the Beef Tallow
  3. Add your diced garlic and onions
  4. Add your ground beef when the garlic and onions started to brown
  5. While waiting for it to boil cut the other vegetables
  6. Add the other vegetables
  7. Simmer for 5mins
  8. Crush the softened tomatoes
  9. Add Salt and Pepper to taste
  10. Put your cooked rice in a bowl
  11. Top it up with the Beef and Tomato Stir-Fry
